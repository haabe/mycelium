# You were handed a Mycelium receipt

**Audience**: someone who received a work receipt made with Mycelium.
**Time to read**: 1 min.
**Last updated**: 2026-06-20.

Someone made the thing you're holding with Mycelium, a product-thinking discipline for AI coding agents that gets them to work out what's worth building before they build it. They thought the work was worth passing to you, which is genuinely the nicest signal this little project gets.

If you want to, come say hi:

→ [Tell us who handed it to you, and what you were building](https://github.com/haabe/mycelium/discussions/new?category=handoffs&title=Someone+handed+me+a+Mycelium+receipt&body=Who+handed+it+to+you%2C+and+what+were+you+building%3F)

That's the whole ask. You don't need to say anything about the tool. Who sent you and what you were working on is the useful part.

Curious what made the receipt? It's generated locally by `/mycelium:receipt-render` from a finished piece of work: the problem as it was framed, what got tested, what got killed, what got kept. Nothing leaves your machine unless you choose to share it.

Mycelium itself lives at [github.com/haabe/mycelium](https://github.com/haabe/mycelium).
